## Content Safety Framework

Nebula Lab is committed to providing safe, compliant AI services with multi-layered security measures.

## Content Moderation

### Professional Moderation System

We use **professional content moderation APIs** for real-time protection:

<Card title="How It Works" icon="shield-check">
  - **Real-time detection**: Every API request is moderated
  - **Smart recognition**: Automatic identification of violations
  - **Active blocking**: Violating content is not returned
  - **Zero tolerance**: Strict measures for violations
</Card>

### Moderation Scope

- **Input moderation**: Check all submitted text and images
- **Output moderation**: Ensure AI-generated content is compliant
- **Multi-language**: Support for Chinese, English and more
- **Multi-modal**: Cover text, images and other content types

## Compliance Requirements

### User Responsibility

<Warning>
Users **must comply with local laws and regulations**, including:
- National and local legal provisions
- Industry regulatory requirements
- International legal standards
- Platform terms of service
</Warning>

### Prohibited Content

The following content types are strictly prohibited:
- **Illegal content**: Information involving illegal activities
- **Harmful information**: Content that may cause harm
- **Inappropriate content**: Information violating social norms
- **Infringing content**: Materials infringing others' rights

## Violation Handling

### Consequences

Nebula Lab reserves the right to take action against violations:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Minor Violations">
    - Content blocking and reminders
    - Usage guidance and warnings
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Repeated Violations">

    - Permission downgrades
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Severe Violations">

</Tabs>
